Process Automation

Custom solutions for recurring tasks, reporting, emails, checks, reminders and internal workflows, aiming to reduce manual work and improve day-to-day organization.

What does automation mean in practice?

Process automation does not necessarily mean that a business needs a large new information system. Very often, the greatest value comes from targeted solutions that organize tasks already performed every day, but which require time, attention and repetition.

Every recurring task can be assessed.

If a task follows specific steps, rules or data inputs, it can be reviewed to determine whether it can be fully or partially automated. The goal is to reduce execution time, limit the risk of errors and provide better visibility over what has been completed and what remains open.

Less timeReduction of manual tasks repeated frequently.
Fewer errorsReduction of errors caused by copy-paste work, data transfers and forgotten actions.
Better controlClear visibility over open items, owners, dates and outputs.
Faster informationReports and dashboards without manual preparation every time.

What can be automated

The following areas are indicative. Each solution is designed based on how the business operates, which files it uses and which points create delays or risk of error.

Excel & Files

Excel and file automations

Data cleansing, file reconciliations, transformations, templates, variance checks and file creation for sending or archiving.

Emails

Bulk and personalized notifications

Emails to clients, suppliers or internal teams using data from Excel, reports or other sources, with tracking and follow-up.

Reporting

Automated reports and Power BI dashboards

Reports and dashboards for sales, collections, expenses, liabilities, productivity, staff, open items and KPIs.

Reminders

Reminders and deadline monitoring

Notifications and tracking lists for deadlines, payments, filings, tasks and internal open items.

Controls

Recurring checks and reconciliations

Checks between files, systems or reports to identify differences, missing values, unexpected amounts or records requiring review.

Workflows

Internal workflows

Structured processes defining who does what, when, with which data and with what result.

Data

Data collection and consolidation

Collection of data from Excel, ERP, CRM, system exports or other files into a single usable format.

Audit Trail

History and audit trail

Recording of actions, dates, deliveries, results and data sources.

Templates

Standardization of files and processes

Stable templates, forms and structured working files for consistency and fewer errors.

Indicative applications by function

Accounting department

Monitoring of obligations, open-item lists, filing tracking, client notifications and file reconciliations.

Management

Dashboards with key indicators, financial overview, open items, cash flows and results.

Sales and collections

Monitoring of customers, offers, invoices, unpaid balances and commercial reports.

Human resources

Monitoring of leaves, attendance, headcount, staff tasks and internal approvals.

Operations

Task organization, deadlines, completion checks and information flow between teams.

Communication

Standardized messages, bulk updates, follow-up lists and communication records.

Identifying automation opportunities

Designing the solution

We design a solution that fits how the business works without adding unnecessary complexity.

Implementation and support

We deliver the solution, explain how it works and support improvements when needed.
