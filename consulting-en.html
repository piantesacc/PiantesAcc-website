Preparation of a complete business plan for banks, investors, funding programs or internal strategic planning.

Company valuation

Support in assessing the value of a business for sale, investor entry, internal planning or restructuring.

Debt restructuring

Out-of-court debt settlement mechanism

Monthly Financial Consulting

Monthly financial monitoring with analysis of results, cash position, key indicators and improvement proposals.

Financial Flows Modelling

Budgeting and Forecasting

Profitability

Cost and profitability analysis

Analysis of costs, margins, products, services or customers to identify improvement opportunities.

Advisory with practical value

Our goal is not simply to create a study or a report, but to provide practical information that helps the business organize decisions, processes and its financial picture more effectively.
